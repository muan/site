The people I worked with, respected, and learned from the most at work before are the people who are now the most wary about LLMs’ growing role in software development. I am not suggesting that because I liked them the most this means they are the most correct. I like them the most because they not only are smart, they also share many of the same values in life with me. 

I am comforted by the fact that we have the same skepticism towards this very controversial development in our field.
